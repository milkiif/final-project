MISSION

MaU Public and External Relation Directorate (PERD) is dedicated to promote and build the image of the University to all its stakeholders by utilizing and developing internal and external communications through different media outlets nationally and internationally.

VISION

MaU Public and External Relation Directorate (PERD) aspires to build an effective team with relevant skills, to create and maintain a climate of trust and positive attitude at Mattu University through honest and effective communication with the international and the local public and thereby improving the University's image by promoting and facilitating transformative flow of information that would potentially maintain a good reputation and excellence of the University.
